About

The as we know it, born in the mid-1980s, has a rich history. Key moments include ARPANET going online in 1969, the first .com domain registration in 1985, and Tim Berners-Lee launching the World Wide Web in 1991. From its humble beginnings to today’s global connectivity, the internet continues to shape our lives profoundly
