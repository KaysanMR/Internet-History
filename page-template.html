The ARPANET

What Was It?


  • ARPANET stands for Advanced Research Projects Agency Network. Quite the mouthful, huh? But don’t worry; it’s more exciting than it sounds.
  • Picture this: It’s the late 1960s, and the United States Department of Defense (specifically, the Advanced Research Projects Agency, or ARPA) is pondering how to connect computers across different locations. They wanted a network that could survive a nuclear attack (because, you know, Cold War vibes).
